Introduction

Crystal Chaos is an online world of fun and fantasy. Follow me and my friends as we save the magical world of Savion.Together we will stop the evil Somacro, king of the goblins. To stop him, we must go on quests to collect the Gems of Peace and Harmony. Let's go on a wild ride!

The retired Questees have the wisdom to tell you what to watch out for and give you shelter. You have the choice to trust or refuse the information and shelter.
